An AI-powered sales forecasting feature

We developed a sales forecasting feature leveraging Meta's Prophet model for accurate predictions. To enhance user understanding, we integrated Gemini AI's generative capabilities to provide detailed suggestions and insightful descriptions. Additionally, we visualized the data through charts and graphs, offering users a clear and comprehensive explanation of the forecasts.
